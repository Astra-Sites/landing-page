Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers! Explore our FAQs to find quick solutions and insights about Astra Softwares, our services, and how we can help you achieve your goals. If you need further assistance, our team is just a message away.

1. How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies based on the complexity and specific requirements of the project. For smaller websites, it can take as little as 2-4 weeks, while larger, custom-built apps may take several months. We’ll provide a detailed timeline during the project consultation.

2. Can you help with SEO optimization?

Yes, we offer SEO optimization services to ensure that your website ranks well in search engines. We also focus on website performance, user experience, and responsiveness to enhance your site’s visibility and functionality..

3. Can you redesign the UI/UX of my existing website?

Absolutely! We specialize in redesigning websites to improve both aesthetics and functionality, enhancing user engagement and satisfaction.

4. What payment methods can be integrated into my website?

We can integrate a wide range of payment solutions, including credit cards, PayPal, Stripe, mobile payments, and local payment gateways.

5. What is the Portfolio Ready Program?

The Portfolio Ready Program is an AI-powered, self-paced web development course that equips you with the skills needed to land a job in the industry. It’s designed for beginners and advanced developers alike, with a focus on building a strong portfolio.
